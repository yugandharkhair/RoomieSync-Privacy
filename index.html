RoomieSync Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 7, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to RoomieSync ('we,' 'our,' or 'us'). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our RoomieSync mobile application (the 'App').

Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Password (securely encrypted)
  • House membership information
  • Status updates and related messages
  • Push notification preferences

Information Automatically Collected:

  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • App usage statistics
  • Crash reports
  • Performance data
  • Installation and first launch data
  • Session duration and frequency of use

Firebase Services Used

  • Firebase Authentication: For user account management
  • Firebase Cloud Firestore: To store user data, house information, and status updates
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: For push notifications
  • Firebase Analytics: To improve app performance and user experience
  • Firebase Crashlytics: To identify and fix app crashes

How We Use Your Information

Core Functionality:

  • Creating and managing user accounts
  • Facilitating house creation and membership
  • Enabling status updates and notifications
  • Managing user interactions within houses

Technical Purposes:

  • Authenticating users
  • Maintaining app security
  • Improving app performance
  • Analyzing usage patterns
  • Debugging and fixing issues

Data Storage and Security

Storage:

  • User data is stored in Firebase Cloud Firestore
  • Authentication data is managed by Firebase Authentication
  • All data is stored on secure Google Cloud Platform servers

Security:

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Secure password hashing
  • Regular security updates
  • Access controls and authentication

Data Retention

We retain your data:

  • While your account is active
  • As required by law
  • For legitimate business purposes

When you delete your account:

  • Personal information is permanently deleted
  • House-related data is anonymized
  • Status updates are removed
  • Authentication data is deleted from Firebase

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Users will be notified of significant changes through:

  • In-app notifications
  • Email notifications
  • App Store updates

Data Protection Rights Under GDPR

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights, including:

  • Access to your data
  • Correction of your data
  • Erasure of your data
  • Data portability
  • Withdrawal of consent

California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under CCPA, including:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of data sales
  • Right to non-discrimination
